Institute of Information Technology, University of Dhaka is an educational institution aimed at developing efficient human resource in the field of information technology. Institute of Information Technology, University of Dhaka (IITDU) is an educational institution aimed at developing efficient human resource in the field of information technology. IIT currently offers Bachelor of Science in Software Engineering (BSSE), Master of Science in Software Engineering (MSSE), Masters in Information Technology (MIT) and Post Graduate Diploma in Information Technology (PGDIT).
